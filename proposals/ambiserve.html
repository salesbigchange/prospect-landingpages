Discovery Meeting

Replacing the "Manual Duplication" Bottleneck

Ambiserve is "slick" at delivery but heavily manual. Key focus areas identified:

  • The Duplication Drain: Information is being manually re-typed from WhatsApp and paper sheets into Xero and office systems.
  • Asset Blindspots: No easy way to track full service history per specific asset (make/model/serial) across your maintenance contracts.
  • Real-Time Professionalism: Moving away from "reading the engineer's handwriting" to instant, branded digital job sheets with embedded photos.
  • Proactive Planning: Shifting from reactionary call-outs to smart, automated maintenance scheduling that builds itself.

Current State vs. Future State

Modernising Ambiserve's field operations.

Current State (Manual & Fragmented)

  • WhatsApp Reliance: Vital job info and photos scattered across messaging threads and JPEGs.
  • Labour Intensive: Office staff spend hours re-typing engineer reports to generate invoices.
  • History Search: Office needs to manually check years of job sheets to find what was done to a specific fridge.
  • Static Planning: Scheduling is done via spreadsheets and manual diaries.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Integrated Ecosystem: Tracking, CRM, Scheduling, and Finance all living in one single source of truth.
  • Instant Invoicing: Job data flows straight from the engineer's tablet to Xero with no re-typing required.
  • Full Asset DNA: QR-code enabled history. Scan the fridge, see every service ever performed instantly.
  • Dynamic Scheduling: Live map tracking and intelligent job allocation based on proximity and skill.
