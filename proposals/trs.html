Current State vs. Future State

Mapping Tank Replacement Services' identified pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with ACT & Paper Diaries

  • Business Risk: Physical paper diaries for each engineer are an "operational nightmare", vulnerable to being lost, damaged, or creating a single point of failure. 1
  • CRM Inefficiency: Using ACT CRM with thousands of unmanageable contacts, leading to staff only using 5% of its capability and creating data silos. 1
  • Manual Quoting & Invoicing: Quotes are created by manually finding and editing old ones. Invoices are taken out physically by engineers, delaying payment. 1
  • Constant Paper Chase: Frequent issues with missing paperwork, job sheets, and service documents cause delays and frustration in the office. 1
  • Wasted Effort: Key processes are done manually "two or three times", leading to significant wasted time and potential for errors. 1

Future State with BigChange

  • Digital & Resilient Planning: A live, cloud-based scheduler provides a single source of truth, accessible anywhere and eliminating the risk of physical diaries.
  • An All-in-One Platform: Replace ACT with a purpose-built CRM that links contacts, sites, assets, jobs, quotes, and invoices together seamlessly.
  • Streamlined Financials: Generate professional quotes from templates in minutes. Create and email invoices directly from the system for faster payments.
  • Instant Digital Paperwork: Engineers complete digital job sheets, risk assessments, and certificates on-site, which are instantly available to the office.
  • Do it Once, Do it Right: A fully integrated system means data is entered once and flows automatically from quote to job to invoice, saving time and reducing errors.

Implementation Plan

A structured, low-risk approach to ensure a smooth transition and rapid time-to-value.

Phase 0: Project Kick-off & Data Preparation (Weeks 1-2)

Configuration of the digital scheduler and the Mobile App for engineers. On-site and remote training for office staff and engineers to replace the paper diaries.
