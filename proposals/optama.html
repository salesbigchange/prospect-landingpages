Current State vs. Future State

Mapping Optama's operational pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with Multiple Systems

  • Fragmented Project Data: Managing surveys, designs, installations, and compliance across spreadsheets and different apps creates confusion and delays.
  • Compliance Burden: Manually compiling photo evidence and checklists for MCS and PAS standards is time-consuming and prone to human error, risking compliance failures.
  • Poor Stock Visibility: No real-time view of high-value stock (panels, inverters, batteries) in the warehouse or allocated to vans, leading to project delays and unnecessary re-ordering.
  • Scheduling Chaos: Coordinating multi-day jobs with different skillsets (roofers, electricians) is a manual, complex process, resulting in inefficient resource use.
  • Inconsistent Customer Experience: Manual communication leads to missed updates, and handover packs are time-intensive to create, impacting customer satisfaction.

Future State with BigChange

  • Unified Project Workflow: A single platform manages the entire job lifecycle from enquiry to compliance, with all data, photos, and notes in one place.
  • Automated Compliance: Mandatory, time-stamped photo capture and digital worksheets on the app ensure all compliance requirements are met on-site, every time.
  • Intelligent Scheduling: A drag-and-drop planner with a live map allows for efficient scheduling of multi-day jobs, optimising routes and resource allocation.
  • Professional Customer Journey: Delight customers with automated ETAs, professional digital job cards, and instantly generated, branded handover packs and certificates.

The Business Case for Optama

A summary of the key drivers for this project, based on our discovery session.

Metrics

Installation Teams: ~15

Multi-stage jobs/week: 25-30

Office Staff: 5

Key Job Types: Solar PV, Battery Storage, EV Chargers

Identified Pains

  • Profit Margin Erosion: Time spent on manual admin and chasing compliance paperwork directly impacts the profitability of each installation.
  • Growth Limitation: Current manual processes are not scalable and will hinder plans to take on more complex commercial projects.
  • Compliance Risk: Any gaps in MCS/PAS evidence gathering can lead to costly rework or delayed payments.
  • Cashflow Delays: Slow creation of handover packs and commissioning documents delays final invoicing and payment.

Decision Criteria

  • A single system to manage the end-to-end renewables installation workflow.
  • Automated and foolproof evidence capture for compliance (MCS, PAS).
  • Live visibility over job progress and scheduling.
  • Improved speed and professionalism of customer communication and documentation.
  • A clear, demonstrable return on investment through efficiency gains.

Phased Implementation Plan for Optama

A structured, partnership-led approach to ensure a smooth transition and rapid time-to-value.

Phase 1: Foundation & Workflow Setup (Weeks 1-4)

We'll work with you to import customer and site data. We will configure your core renewables workflow, including custom job sheets for surveys, installations, and compliance to meet MCS/PAS standards.

Phase 2: Pilot Group Go-Live (Weeks 5-8)

Roll out BigChange to a select group of 2-3 installation teams. This allows for focused training, real-world testing of the compliance workflows, and gathering feedback before a company-wide launch.

Phase 3: Full Rollout & Business Review (Weeks 9-12)

Using lessons from the pilot, we'll train and roll out to the remaining teams. We will conduct a business review to measure initial KPIs and ROI.
