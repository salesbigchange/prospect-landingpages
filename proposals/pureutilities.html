Current State with WorkEva & Paper

  • Paper-Based Job Packs: A full-time employee manually creates and prints 150+ job packs per day. Evidence photos are sent separately via WhatsApp.

  • Duplicate Effort: Separate departments (e.g., Jetting) use different manual job boards, creating total lack of visibility and duplicated effort.

Future State with BigChange

  • 100% Digital Workflow: Jobs are created in seconds and sent instantly to the engineer's device, complete with all site history. Evidence photos are attached directly to the job.
