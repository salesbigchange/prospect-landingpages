Current State vs. Future State

Moving from "Excel & Email" to a system that manages the workload for you.

Current State (Overload)

  • Office Burnout: Back office staff working evenings and weekends just to keep up with the manual admin.
  • Report Errors: Engineers using Pages on iPhones leading to incorrect dates and "see attached photos" with no attachments.
  • Communication Cracks: Jobs and updates lost in email threads and WhatsApp messages.
  • Zero Visibility: No way to know if a job is actually done until the engineer emails in. No tracking of the 16 vehicles.
  • Manual Scheduling: Engineers manage their own diaries, making ad-hoc call-outs hard to schedule efficiently.

Future State (Control)

  • Automated Workflows: Recurring PPM jobs (e.g., Restaurant Chains) auto-populate, reducing data entry by 90%.
  • Mandatory Compliance: Engineers must take photos and sign off before closing a job. No more empty reports.
  • Real-Time Visibility: See exactly where your 16 engineers are and the status of every job (Red/Green) instantly.
  • Smart Scheduling: Optimized routing and visibility of who is nearest for emergency call-outs.
  • Offline Reliability: Native app works perfectly in basements or areas with poor signal, syncing automatically later.
