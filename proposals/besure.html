Current State vs. Future State

Mapping BeSure's operational pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with Multiple Systems

  • Fragmented Data Silos: Using multiple, non-integrated Excel spreadsheets for different clients makes tracking jobs difficult and time-consuming.
  • Manual, Inefficient Scheduling: Supervisors physically arrange paper job sheets into piles each morning for operatives to collect, a process that is not scalable or flexible.
  • Lack of Real-Time Visibility: The office has no live information on job progress, only finding out about issues or completions when paper sheets are returned the next day.
  • Delayed Problem Solving: Issues like "no access" or incorrect phone numbers aren't known until the next day, delaying resolution and impacting customer satisfaction.
  • Growth Limitation: Current paper and spreadsheet processes are a barrier to growth, especially when looking to expand into new areas like London.

Future State with BigChange

  • A Single Source of Truth: One central system to manage all clients and jobs, from quote to invoice, eliminating data fragmentation and confusion.
  • Dynamic Digital Scheduling: Jobs are sent directly to operatives' devices. Planners can see live availability and drag-and-drop jobs into diaries, optimising routes and workloads.
  • Complete Job Visibility: Live status updates (traveling, on-site, complete, issue) provide the office with a real-time view of the day's progress, enabling proactive management.
  • Instant Communication: Operatives report issues directly from site via the app, allowing the office to react immediately and reschedule or contact the client.
  • Scalable Foundations: A robust digital infrastructure that provides the control and efficiency needed to manage growth and take on more work confidently.

Phased Implementation Plan for BeSure

A structured, partnership-led approach to ensure a smooth transition and rapid time-to-value.

Phase 1: Foundation & Data Setup (Weeks 1-4)

We'll work with you to import your client and site data from Excel. We will then configure your core workflows and build your primary job sheets for disabled adaptations and reactive maintenance tasks.

Phase 2: Pilot Group Go-Live (Weeks 5-8)

Roll out BigChange to a select group of 3-4 operatives. This allows for focused training and real-world testing of the job management process, gathering crucial feedback before a company-wide launch.

Phase 3: Full Rollout & Financial Integration (Weeks 9-12)

Using lessons from the pilot, we'll train and roll out to the remaining teams. The full Sage 50 integration will be enabled, and we will conduct a business review to measure initial KPIs and ROI.
