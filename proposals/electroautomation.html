Meeting 1: Discovery

Moving on from PSI & WorkPal

Attendees: Kellie-Anne (Implementation), Stephen (Installation Mgr), Tom (Consultant), Scott, Serge.

Electro Automation needs to replace PSI (linked to Opera) before it becomes obsolete. Key pain points identified:

  • PSI End-of-Life: The current system only supports Android versions older than v13, creating a hardware crisis for new engineers.
  • Asset Intelligence: You need deep asset history (not just site history) to track specific barriers, gates, and lifts to justify replacements.
  • Hybrid Workflows: You need a system that handles high-volume PPM/Callouts and multi-day Installation projects.
  • Data Visibility: A strong need for custom dashboards (Power BI) to visualize profitability and stock.

Current State vs. Future State

From fragmented, legacy systems to a unified, cloud-based platform.

Current State (PSI / WorkPal)

  • Hardware Crisis: PSI is incompatible with modern Android devices (v13+), blocking hardware upgrades.
  • Limited Asset Data: "Site history" exists, but true "Asset history" is lacking, making it hard to track individual barrier performance.
  • Fragmented Systems: Operations split across PSI (Republic) and WorkPal (NI/UK), preventing a unified view.
  • Manual Follow-ups: If a job isn't finished, the follow-on job creation process is manual and prone to being missed.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Device Agnostic: Works seamlessly on any modern Android/iOS device, removing hardware headaches.
  • Asset DNA: Scan a barcode to see the full service history, cost-to-serve, and uptime of that specific asset.
  • One Truth: A single platform for Republic, NI, and UK operations, scalable to 80+ licenses.
  • Smart Automation: "Completed with Issues" automatically triggers a follow-on job and alerts the service desk.
