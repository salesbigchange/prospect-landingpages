Your Roadmap to Success

We understand that moving from 7+ systems (including Swiftcase, Monday.com, and AssetGo) is a significant change. We have built a dedicated onboarding plan to ensure RMLFS sees value from day one.

Step 1: Kick-off & Alignment

Project Initiation

We start with a dedicated kick-off meeting with your project leads (Jack and Stuart) to map out the exact scope.

  • Define the "Golden Thread" from quote to invoice tailored for RMLFS.
  • Agree on the timeline for the 20+ engineers and office staff.
  • Review current pain points with Monday.com and Swiftcase to ensure they are addressed immediately.
  • Project Portal Access: Gain immediate access to our dedicated onboarding portal to track progress, view workshop schedules, and access preliminary resources before the main scope call.
  • BigChange University (LMS): Immediate, unlimited access to our self-paced Learning Management System for ongoing staff certification and upskilling on new features.
Step 2: Data Migration

Extract & Import

We take the heavy lifting out of moving your data. We will assist in extracting clean data from your legacy systems.

  • Customers & Sites: Import all commercial sites (e.g., Homebase, B&M) with accurate geofencing.
  • Stock & Assets: Migrate data from AssetGo into BigChange.
  • Financials: Ensure seamless mapping for the Xero integration.
Step 3: Configuration (The Build)

Tailoring the System

We configure BigChange to mirror RMLFS's specific workflows, not the other way around.

  • Pre-Builds: Set up "Pre-build" job types for Roofing, Maintenance, and Reactive works to speed up quoting.
  • Mandatory Checks: Configure the mobile app to force vehicle checks and dynamic risk assessments before a job can start (as discussed with Stuart).
  • Templates: Build your branded Job Cards, Invoices, and Quotes.
Step 4: Training

Empowering Your Team

A blended approach to ensure everyone is confident before we go live.

  • Office Training: Deep dive for Jack, Mike, and David on Scheduling, CRM, and Financial reporting.
  • "Train the Trainer": We will focus intensely on Stuart (your Champion) so he can lead the rollout to the engineers.
  • Engineer Sessions: Practical, hands-on sessions for the mobile app to ensure 100% adoption.
Step 5: Go-Live & Hypercare

Launch Day Support

We don't just switch it on and leave. We provide dedicated support during the transition.

  • Go-Live Standby: Dedicated support on the day of switchover to handle immediate queries.
  • Floorwalking: Option for on-site presence to assist office staff and engineers in real-time.
  • Post-Go-Live Review: A review meeting 4 weeks later to optimize workflows and ensure ROI is being met.
