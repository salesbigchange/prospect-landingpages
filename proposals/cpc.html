Current State vs. Future State

Mapping CPC's operational pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with Multiple Systems

  • Fragmented Operations: Juggling Jotform for communication, Fixflo for reactive jobs, and Masternaut for tracking creates data silos and inefficient workflows.
  • Missed Revenue & Disputes: With ~2,000 Jotform submissions a month, missed visits are hard to track, leading to lost revenue and an inability to prove attendance during client disputes.
  • Outdated Client Communication: Client reports are seen as less professional than competitors', requiring manual clicks and attachments, damaging brand reputation.
  • Lack of Insight: No integrated way to track time-on-site vs. quoted time, leading to unprofitable contracts and missed opportunities for upselling.
  • Reactive Management: Without a central system, managers can't see if a scheduled job has been missed until a complaint comes in, forcing a reactive and defensive approach.

Future State
with BigChange

  • One Unified Platform: All planned maintenance, reactive repairs, scheduling, tracking, and client communication are managed in a single, seamless system.
  • Guaranteed Proof of Service: Every scheduled job must be actioned on the app. Missed visits are instantly flagged, eliminating lost revenue and providing concrete evidence of attendance.
  • Full Profitability Analysis: Track actual time on site against scheduled duration. Reports highlight under-billed contracts, enabling data-driven repricing and improved profitability.
  • Proactive Job Management: A live schedule and real-time alerts provide full visibility. Managers can instantly see late or missed jobs, allowing for proactive intervention before it becomes a client issue.
  • Elevated Customer Journey: Delight clients with automated visit reminders and "en-route" text messages, complete with an "Uber-style" map to track their team's arrival.
  • Client Self-Service Portal: Offer a professional, branded portal where clients can view job reports, see upcoming appointments, and access historical service data 24/7.

The Business Case for CPC

A summary of the key drivers for this project, based on our discovery session.

Metrics

Field Teams: ~60

Reactive Repair Teams: 5

Sites: Several hundred+

Monthly Submissions: ~2,000 Jotforms

Identified Pains

  • Revenue Leakage: Inability to track all 2,000 monthly visits leads to missed jobs, resulting in having to "give the money back" during disputes.
  • Reputational Risk: Client feedback indicates current reporting methods are outdated compared to competitors, impacting brand image.
  • Operational Inefficiency: Juggling multiple systems for jobs, communication, and tracking is cumbersome and prevents a unified view of operations.
  • Lack of Profit Insight: No easy way to compare time spent on site vs. quoted time, leading to unprofitable contracts going unnoticed.

Decision Criteria

  • A single, all-in-one solution to replace Jotform, Fixflo, and Masternaut.
  • Guaranteed, reportable proof of attendance for every scheduled job.
  • Professional, automated client communication and reporting.
  • Ability to report on profitability and time-on-site vs. planned.
  • A practical, cost-effective solution that delivers clear business benefits.

Phased Implementation Plan for CPC

A structured, partnership-led approach to ensure a smooth transition and rapid time-to-value.

Phase 1: Foundation & Data Setup (Weeks 1-4)

We'll work with you to export client, site, and contract data. We will configure core system settings, users, and build your primary grounds maintenance and cleaning worksheets.

Phase 2: Pilot Group Go-Live (Weeks 5-8)

Roll out BigChange to a single region or a select group of ~10-15 teams. This allows for focused training, real-world testing of workflows, and gathering feedback before a company-wide launch.

Phase 3: Full Rollout & Financial Integration (Weeks 9-12)

Using lessons from the pilot, we'll train and roll out to the remaining teams. The full Sage 50 integration will be enabled, and we will conduct a business review to measure initial KPIs and ROI.
