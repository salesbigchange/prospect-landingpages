Your "Simple V1" Solution

Moving from a system that creates problems to a simple, digital platform that solves them.

Current State: Paper, Post & Xero

  • Disconnected Diaries: Ryan and Carl use separate paper diaries, creating scheduling conflicts and zero visibility for management.
  • Email & Post Job Sheets: Job details are emailed, but completed handwritten reports are physically posted back to the office, causing huge delays.
  • Lost Revenue: Posted job sheets "potentially get lost," meaning jobs are completed but can never be invoiced, resulting in 100% lost revenue.
  • Manual Data Re-Entry: Office staff manually re-type all information from the paper report into Xero to generate an invoice.
  • No Team Visibility: Admin staff like Lucy and Sheila cannot see the schedule or job status, creating a constant bottleneck for information.

Future State with BigChange (V1)

  • One Central, Live Diary: Ryan and Carl share one digital diary, visible from anywhere. They can both schedule jobs, and drag-and-drop to reschedule in seconds.
  • Instant Digital Job Sheets: Jobs are sent instantly to the technician's tablet. The digital report is completed on-site and sent back to the office the moment the job is finished.
  • Zero Lost Paperwork: 100% of completed job reports are received instantly and digitally, ensuring every job can be invoiced and eliminating lost revenue.
  • Seamless Xero Integration: Completed job data flows directly to Xero, allowing for instant, accurate invoice creation with no re-keying of data.
  • Configurable Permissions: Ryan and Carl have full control to move the diary, while other office staff can be given 'view-only' access to see job progress without being able to make changes.

Your Blueprint for a Scalable Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, starting simple and scaling with you.

The Simple, Shared Diary

The Pain: Disconnected paper diaries. We solve your #1 pain. A single, live, cloud-based diary that both Ryan and Carl can access and update from anywhere, eliminating all scheduling conflicts and confusion.

Instant, Digital Reports

The Pain: Lost paper and postal delays. Technicians (like Carl, when he works for Contego) use the simple app to complete digital reports. The moment they hit 'complete', the office has the report, ready for invoicing.

Seamless Xero Integration

The Pain: Manual invoicing. Our platinum-partner Xero integration syncs customers and allows completed jobs to be pushed directly into Xero, ready to be invoiced. This eliminates re-keying and saves hours of admin time.

Granular User Permissions

The Pain: "I don't want anyone to mess it up." We solve this by giving Ryan and Carl full "Super User" rights to move the diary, while admin staff can have 'View Only' or restricted access to prevent accidental changes.

Simple, Flexible Scheduling

The Pain: Jobs moving or going sideways. A job can be dragged and dropped to another technician or another day in seconds. If a job is cancelled, it goes back into the 'unscheduled' pot, ready to be re-booked without re-typing details.

No-Risk Implementation
