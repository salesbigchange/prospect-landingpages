Current State vs. Future State

Moving from manual workarounds to a single source of truth.

Current State (Manual & Disconnected)

  • Planning Silos: Planning done in a 10-year-old temporary tool that doesn't talk to the field.
  • Reactive Service: Customers have to call to find out when crews will arrive (ETA anxiety).
  • Paperwork Chase: RAMS and Site Surveys sent via email/PDF; no easy way to confirm receipt or completion.
  • Stock Blindspots: Difficult to track third-party haulage arrival versus internal crew arrival seamlessly.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Integrated Ecosystem: Jobs flow from G42 to BigChange automatically (API), reducing double entry.
  • Air Traffic Control: Live "Uber-style" tracking links for customers and "Late Start" alerts for the office.
  • Digital Compliance: Forced workflows (Vehicle Checks, RAMS) before a job can even start.
  • Smart Resource Management: Manage multi-crew "Group Jobs" and third-party haulage in one view.

The Business Case for TPA

Why modernizing operations drives value beyond just software.

Revenue & Efficiency

Stop the Chase: Eliminate the need for the office team to chase crews for updates, freeing them to focus on complex logistics.

Proof of Service: Capture 50+ photos per job (install/recovery) for indisputable evidence against damage claims or disputes.

Asset & Stock Control

Master Ticket Automation: Automatically collate data from multiple crews (e.g., 400 panels installed) into one "Master Ticket" for customer sign-off.

Stock Visibility: Track exactly when kit arrives on site via third-party haulage vs internal vehicles.

Operational Wins

  • Forced Compliance: Crews cannot start a job without completing a vehicle check and Point of Work Risk Assessment.
  • Smart Scheduling: "Air Traffic Control" view warns of late starts or traffic delays before the customer even notices.
  • Offline Capability: Crews can work seamlessly even on remote sites with no signal.
