Eliminating the Bottlenecks

Hi Tom and Liam, it was great to dive into the operations at TPA. It's clear that as the central hub of supply and logistics, your team is currently managing high complexity with tools that were meant to be temporary a decade ago.


BigChange is designed to take the weight off your shoulders by automating the manual "load sheets," providing real-time visibility on haulage, and giving your crews everything they need in one single mobile app. We are ready to help TPA become the "slick and easy" operation you envision.

Critical Pain Points Identifed

Reactive Firefighting

Escalation points occur constantly when people don't turn up or breakdowns happen. Managing these manually via email and phone calls creates a bottleneck in the central office.

Manual Paperwork Loops

Load sheets, Rams documents, and site surveys are currently handled via Excel mail merges and PDFs in emails. This leads to information gaps and slow feedback from the road.

The Visibility Gap

Crews on-site lack visibility on when external haulage arrives. Customer "Where are you?" calls are a constant drain, especially on Monday mornings when crews are traveling from workshops.

The BigChange Solution

Air Traffic Control

Full 360-degree visibility of every vehicle, crew, and job. Proactive alerts tell you a job will be late before it even happens, allowing you to manage customer expectations instantly.

Digital Workflows

Crews receive their full week's work, site surveys, and Rams on their device. Point-of-work risk assessments are mandatory, date-stamped, and photos are instantly shared back to the office.

Seamless Integration

By connecting with your in-house G42 system, we eliminate double entry. Jobs flow into BigChange, and completion data flows back to your stock management automatically.
