Current State vs. Future State

Moving from a rigid system with heavy workarounds to a flexible, all-in-one platform.

Current State with Simpro

  • Stock Management Issues: Significant challenges with managing and moving stock items from the mobile app, creating data inaccuracies and inefficiencies.
  • Rigid & Difficult Scheduling: Lack of smart scheduling capabilities makes booking new installations and repair appointments a difficult, manual process.
  • Unsupported & Unresponsive: Non-existent support from the customer success team has left you to find your own workarounds and solutions.
  • Painful Reporting: Heavy lifting is required outside of the system to generate the necessary reports and KPIs, wasting valuable time and resources.

Future State with BigChange

  • Flexible Stock Control: A highly configurable stock system with robust API capabilities, allowing for seamless integration and accurate, real-time tracking of high-value items.
  • Intelligent & Smart Scheduling: Advanced scheduling tools that suggest the most cost-effective engineer for a job, with the ability to bulk-schedule hundreds of jobs in minutes.
  • A True Partnership: A dedicated, UK-based support and success team that works with you to configure the system and provides 24/7 assistance.
  • Powerful, Instant Reporting: Out-of-the-box dashboards and a custom report builder to get the data you need, when you need it, without manual manipulation.

The Business Case for Change

Key drivers for selecting a new strategic platform partner.

Metrics

Install Types: Standard, Non-Standard, and Complex (underground installs).

Operations: Network builds (cabinet & joint work) and customer installations.

Initial Scale: 200 users, with a need for a flexible, scalable model.

Decision Makers

Key Stakeholders: Mark Kelly (Field Engineering Director), Chardy Sharpe-Fox (Operations Lead), James Blessing (Head of Development), with a need to present a robust commercial case.

Key Outcomes: A flexible, highly-customisable platform that can handle complex install scenarios, provide accurate stock control for high-value items, and offer intelligent scheduling to improve efficiency.

Decision Criteria

  • A platform with superior flexibility and configurability compared to Simpro.
  • Robust API for seamless integration with stock management and other systems.
  • Smart scheduling tools to automate and optimise appointment booking.
  • A strong, reliable support and partnership model.
  • Ability to lock in long-term contracts (e.g., 5 years) for commercial stability.
