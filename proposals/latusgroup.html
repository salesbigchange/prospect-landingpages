Current State with eWorks Manager

  • Manual "Copy & Paste" Jobs: All "Closed-Won" deals from HubSpot must be manually re-keyed into eWorks, wasting hours and risking data entry errors.
  • Rigid Scheduling: Cannot handle the complexity of 100 technicians with varying skills, locations (hotels vs. home), or client-specific technician preferences.
  • Manual Reporting: Key metrics like technician utilisation and "look forward" reports are created manually in spreadsheets, costing valuable time.
  • Manual Client Confirmations: Your team manually calls every single job the day before, a time-consuming process that still results in missed appointments.
  • Tracking & Lateness Issues: Poor mobile tracking (especially on iOS) and no proactive alerts for technicians running late, forcing your team to be reactive.

Future State with BigChange

  • Seamless HubSpot Integration: A "Closed-Won" deal in HubSpot *automatically* creates a fully-populated job in BigChange, ready for scheduling. This eliminates all manual data entry.
  • Intelligent, Complex Scheduling: Our smart scheduler finds the best tech based on skills, client preference ("only send this person"), and real-time location (including hotels).
  • Live, Automated Dashboards: See real-time utilisation for all 100 techs, broken down by van vs. room, area, or team. Instantly see your forward-booked capacity.
  • Automated Client Comms: Eliminate manual confirmation calls. Send automated branded reminders and "on-the-way" alerts with live ETAs, reducing no-shows and freeing up your bookers.
  • Proactive Lateness Alerts: Get an instant, automatic alert if a technician is running late, allowing your team to manage the client relationship proactively, not reactively.

Your Blueprint for an Automated Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, feature by feature.

HubSpot Integration (No More Copy-Paste)

The Pain: "Duplicating our work". We'll connect HubSpot to BigChange. When your sales team closes a deal, the job is created instantly in the schedule, ready for Lucy's team, eliminating all manual data entry.

Intelligent, Complex Scheduling

The Pain: "eWorks can't handle the complexity". Our 'Smart Scheduler' finds the best tech based on skills ("good enough for this client"), real-time location (including hotels), and van vs. room-based setups.

Automated Utilisation Reports

The Pain: Manual spreadsheet reporting. Stop wasting time. Instantly see live utilisation for all 100 techs, broken down by area, team, or user type. We also offer Power BI integration for ultimate data control.

Automated Client Comms

The Pain: Daily manual confirmation calls. Eliminate this task. Our system automatically sends branded email/SMS reminders and "on-the-way" alerts with live ETAs, reducing no-shows and freeing up your bookers.

Reliable iOS & Android App

The Pain: Poor mobile tracking. Our native app provides reliable location tracking on both iOS and Android. It also provides a single hub for techs to complete jobs, fill forms, and manage their day, online or offline.

Proactive Lateness Alerts

The Pain: Reacting to complaints. Don't wait for a client to call. BigChange uses live traffic and job data to send your team an automatic alert if a technician is running late, so you can manage the situation proactively.
