Our Understanding

apartment

Social Housing Specialists

Cato Services is a UK facilities management contractor whose core business is high-volume, quick-turnaround work for social housing clients, including responsive repairs, voids, and damp & mould jobs.

construction

A Growing Workforce

You currently have a team of 40 operatives on the road, supported by 12 office staff. This number is set to grow, increasing the pressure on your current operational systems.

build_circle

Using a Project-Focused System

Your current system, Simpro, is built for large, complex projects. This creates unnecessary complexity and clunky workflows for your fast-paced, jobbing-focused social housing contracts.

Pain & Challenges with Simpro

mobile_off

Mobile App is Too Complex

The Simpro app is cumbersome, with too many taps, screens, and confusing options. This frustrates your operatives and leads to poor adoption and inconsistent data capture.

layers_clear

Clunky, Multi-Layered Admin

The back-office system is rigid and forces you through unnecessary layers of admin. Simple tasks like booking multiple jobs are "hell," and you can't simplify the process.

receipt_long

Duplicated Purchasing Process

The process for handling purchases and bills feels duplicated, requiring operatives or office staff to receipt items multiple times before they can be exported to Xero, wasting time and creating confusion.

person_alert

Scheduling Lacks Intelligence

Scheduling relies on planners' manual knowledge rather than intelligent, system-driven logic based on operatives' skills, real-time location, and availability.

Aims & Business Goals

phone_iphone

Dramatically Simplify the Mobile App

Implement an "evidence-first" mobile workflow with an incredibly simple UI, large buttons, and a "two-tap" rule to get to any critical action, ensuring high adoption and perfect data capture.

model_training

Streamline Back-Office Workflows

Move to a flexible, configurable back-office system that allows you to remove unnecessary admin layers and automate status changes based on real-world events.

dynamic_feed

Implement Intelligent Scheduling

Utilise a system that schedules based on operative skills, geographic zones, and availability, reducing travel time and ensuring the right person is sent to every job.

rocket_launch

Adopt a Platform Built for Jobbing

Switch to a system designed from the ground up for high-volume, quick-turnaround reactive maintenance and social housing jobs, not complex, long-term projects.

ROI & Driving Value

engineering

Increase Operative Productivity by 20%

A simpler app means less time fighting the system and more time on the tools. Saving just 15 minutes per job on a 10-job day frees up over 2 hours for more billable work.

schedule

Reduce Admin Time by 8+ Hours Per Week

By eliminating clunky job loading, duplicated purchasing, and manual scheduling, your 12 office staff can save over a full working day each week, focusing on service instead of admin.

check_circle

Improve First-Time Fix Rate

Intelligent scheduling ensures the operative with the right skills is always sent first. Forced-step evidence capture guarantees they have all the info, reducing the need for costly return visits.

support_agent

Enhance Tenant Communication & Satisfaction

Automated "on the way" alerts and professional communication tools reduce no-access appointments and improve the overall service experience for social housing tenants.
