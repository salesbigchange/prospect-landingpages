Current State vs. Future State

Transforming Advanced Damp Ltd from app overload to streamlined efficiency.

Current State (Fragmented)

  • System Overload: Managing Monday.com, SafetyCulture, HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Quartix, and Excel separately.
  • Manual Automations: Monday.com isn't built for construction; you're manually clicking to trigger "automations" and building workarounds.
  • Data Disconnect: Surveyors do reports in SafetyCulture, download them, then attach to Monday. Estimating is done separately in Excel.
  • "Headless Chickens": Staff running around trying to piece together information creates operational chaos and miscommunication.
  • Learning Curve: New employees struggle to learn the complex, custom Monday.com setup, leading to missed steps.

Future State (Unified)

  • One Truth: CRM, Job Scheduling, Vehicle Tracking (replacing Quartix), and Financials in one construction-specific platform.
  • True Automation: Workflows that actually flow. From lead -> damp survey -> quote -> job sheet -> invoice without re-keying data.
  • Seamless Surveys: Surveyors capture data (photos, moisture readings) directly in the app, which instantly populates professional quote templates.
  • Forced Compliance: Mandatory RAMS and vehicle checks on the app before work starts. No more chasing forms.
  • Subcontractor Friendly: Use the Subcontractor App (£1/job) for your flexible technician workforce.
