Current State vs. Future State

Mapping Retrofit Scotland's challenges to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with Manual Systems

  • Disconnected Tools: Managing projects using a combination of Dropbox for files, ServiceM8 for installers, and Excel spreadsheets for scheduling leads to chaotic workflows. 1
  • Lost Leads & Follow-ups: Leads from previous months are easily lost in trackers, with no automated reminders or live status updates, causing missed opportunities and delays. 2
  • Manual Compliance & Evidence: Collecting and tracking ECO4 compliance evidence is a manual process, relying on separate folders and checklists, increasing the risk of errors.
  • Lack of Project Profitability: No clear, real-time way to track costs vs. funding for each project, making it difficult to identify which jobs are profitable until after the fact. 3
  • Reactive Communication: Chasing surveyors and installers for updates and evidence relies on texts and calls, with no central system to log communication and track progress.

Future State with BigChange

  • One Unified Platform: Manage the entire ECO4 journey from lead generation to final submission in a single system, eliminating spreadsheets and disconnected apps.
  • Live Lead & Project Tracking: Never lose a lead again. A live CRM with customisable flags and automated reminders ensures every opportunity is tracked and actioned.
  • Automated Compliance Workflows: Build fully configurable, mandatory workflows for surveyors and installers to capture all required evidence, photos, and signatures for PAS 2030 compliance.
  • Real-Time Profit & Loss: Instantly see the profitability of every project. Track material costs, labour, and subcontractor fees against funding to ensure every job is profitable.
  • Streamlined Team Collaboration: Assign tasks, set deadlines, and communicate within the platform. Automated notifications keep everyone in the loop, from the office to the field.

Phased Implementation Plan

A structured, low-risk approach to ensure a smooth transition and rapid time-to-value.

Phase 0: Process Mapping & Data Prep (Weeks 1-2)

Collaborative workshops to map your entire lead-to-submission process. We'll assist your team in exporting customer and lead data for a clean import.

Phase 1: Core Workflow Go-Live (Weeks 3-6)

Configuration of CRM, lead tracking, and survey workflows. On-site and remote training for office staff and surveyors on the new, automated processes.

Phase 2: Installation & Financials (Weeks 7-9)

Building installer workflows, purchase order management, and project profitability tracking. Training for the installation and finance teams.

Phase 3: Automation & Reporting (Weeks 10-12)

Implementing advanced automations (e.g., status updates, alerts for missing evidence) and building custom reports for ABS scores and funder submissions.
