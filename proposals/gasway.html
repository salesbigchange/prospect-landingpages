Your Challenges & Our Solutions

How BigChange directly addresses the operational bottlenecks impacting Gasway's electrical and renewables team.

Pain: The "Gabriel" Bottleneck

Gabriel lacks geo-tagging capabilities, making PAS/MCS compliance incredibly difficult and manual. Updating forms takes months of development.

BigChange Solution

Create and update dynamic worksheets in seconds. Photos are automatically geo-stamped and time-stamped without needing third-party apps like Solocator.

Pain: Manual Photo Labeling

Engineers take 30-40 photos per commissioning job, often forgetting to label them. Office staff spend hours sorting them into folders for lodgement.

BigChange Solution

Force engineers to take pre-labeled photos via mandatory worksheet prompts. The final PDF report is automatically formatted, labeled, and ready for Easy MCS.

Pain: Disjointed Workflow

Van stock is managed via Excel, and data transfer between field staff and group systems is slow and resource-heavy.

BigChange Solution

Live van stock visibility, automated part requests, and the ability to set up automated email routing for job reports (with future scope for full API integration by our team).

The ROI for Gasway

By adopting BigChange alongside your existing infrastructure, your renewables and electrical teams can instantly modernize their operations without waiting years for a group-level ERP rollout.

  • Hours Saved Daily: Eliminate the manual download, renaming, and filing of photos for Easy MCS.
  • 100% Compliance Accuracy: Mandatory fields ensure engineers cannot close a job without the exact required evidence.
  • Tailored Pricing: As discussed, a targeted deployment focused purely on job management and mobile execution, keeping costs well within local budget sign-off.
