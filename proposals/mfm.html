From Clik to BigChange: Streamlined Job Management for MFM Group

A strategic proposal to transition MFM Group from an on-premise server to a resilient, all-in-one cloud platform—featuring full offline mobile capability, automated client communications, integrated certification, and seamless timesheet reconciliation.

Current State vs. Future State

Mapping MFM Group's identified pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with Clik

  • Server & Business Risk: On-premise servers are ~7 years old, recently failed causing half a day of downtime, and cost ~£20k to replace. Qlik software has been acquired and faces an 'end-of-life' future. 1
  • Operational Friction: Timesheet process is a manual "bugbear," requiring days of admin effort to reconcile. Engineers must input times for every job, leading to errors. 2
  • Disconnected Processes: Gas certification is managed in a separate app (Ink Works), and electrical tests use Excel spreadsheets, creating data silos and manual PDF handling. 3
  • Manual Quoting: Creating a quote is a multi-step, manual process involving Word documents, PDF merging, and manual emailing, taking significant admin time. 4
  • Limited Client Visibility: Client communication is reactive. Providing ETAs and job updates requires manual phone calls and emails, consuming helpdesk time. 5

Future State with BigChange

  • Cloud-Native Resilience: A fully managed SaaS platform eliminates server hardware costs, failure risks, and maintenance overhead, ensuring business continuity.
  • Automated Timesheets: Time is captured automatically when engineers start/finish jobs via the mobile app. This is reconciled against vehicle tracking data, virtually eliminating admin.
  • All-in-One Field App: Engineers complete jobs, risk assessments, and industry-standard certificates (e.g., Gas Safety) within a single app that works fully offline.
  • Integrated CRM-to-Invoice: Generate professional quotes from templates, get e-signatures, and convert to jobs in a few clicks, drastically reducing admin and eliminating manual document creation.
  • Proactive Customer Experience: Automated alerts (SMS/email) with live tracking links ("engineer on the way") reduce inbound calls and delight customers. A client portal provides 24/7 access to job history and documents.

Phased Implementation Plan

A structured, low-risk approach to ensure a smooth transition and rapid time-to-value.

Phase 0: Project Kick-off & Data Preparation (Weeks 1-2)

Collaborative workshops to confirm workflows. We'll assist your team in exporting customer, site, and asset data from Clik into our import templates.

Phase 1: Core System Go-Live (Weeks 3-6)

Configuration of CRM, Job Scheduling, and the Mobile App. On-site and remote training for office staff and engineers. BigChange tracking hardware installed across your fleet.

Phase 2: Advanced Configuration & Automation (Weeks 7-9)

Building custom worksheets, reports, and alerts based on MFM's specific needs (e.g., quote required notifications). Setup of Client Portal and automated communications.

Phase 3: Financial Integration & Review (Weeks 10-12)

Once MFM migrates from Opera to a supported accounting package (e.g., Sage), we will enable the full financial integration. Business review to measure initial KPIs and ROI.
