Discovery & Goals

Escaping Fragmented Data & Legacy Bottlenecks

CTS Security aims to modernise, but existing legacy systems and spreadsheet reliance are limiting operational control. Key pain points identified:

  • Compliance Risks: Missing clarity on upgrade scope, system grading, BS standards, and URNs during the quoting process.
  • Poor Visibility: Poor visibility of engineer activities and missing timestamps affecting SLA compliance. Asset data is scattered.
  • Scheduling Friction: Clunky scheduling systems require manual locking/unlocking of jobs, with poor visibility of engineer availability.
  • Invoicing Issues: Manual invoice tracking, disconnected PO management, and complex pricing structures leading to rejected or missed invoices.

Current State vs. Future State

Moving from manual workarounds to a single source of truth.

Current State (Mentor/CASH)

  • Spreadsheet Reliance: Site records, specifications, and purchasing are disconnected across manual documents.
  • Data Vacuum: Missing timestamps, unmanaged asset histories, and manual invoice tracking cause blindspots.
  • Clunky Operations: Complex manual setups for PPMs, battery replacements, and engineer scheduling.
  • Compliance Risks: NSI standards and critical insurer requirements are not consistently captured during quoting.

Future State (Simpro)

  • Single Source of Truth: One platform for sites, individual asset hierarchies, purchasing, and invoicing.
  • Automated Compliance: Pre-built templates enforce URN, NSI, and BS standard data capture at the quoting stage.
  • Engineer Empowerment: Mobile app ensures real-time logging with voice-to-text, photo uploads, and mandatory workflows.
  • Smart Scheduling: Drag-and-drop, map-based scheduling provides real-time visibility of engineers and auto-schedules PPMs.
