Current State: Spreadsheets & Disconnected Apps

  • Fragmented Operations: Managing all processes via spreadsheets. This is "not very efficient, but manageable" now, but will not support your growth plans.
  • Manual Timesheets & Payroll: A huge admin drain. "one or two full-time roles" are wasted manually collecting and processing timesheets for complex, multi-rate pay structures (onshore/offshore, day/night).
  • "CRM in a Spreadsheet": Your multi-year tender process is managed on a spreadsheet, with no automation, follow-up tracking, or clear pipeline visibility.
  • Clunky Compliance: Critical H&S and skills data is siloed in AdeptSoft, which is "not user friendly" and "easy to delete things from," creating significant risk.
  • Disconnected Forms: You use iAuditor for digital forms, but it's a separate system that doesn't link to your jobs, schedule, or operatives' compliance records.

Future State with BigChange

  • One Integrated Platform: BigChange acts as the core operational arm of your new ERP, providing one source of truth for jobs, operatives, and compliance.
  • Automated Timesheet Costing: Capture time on-site via the app. Our automation builder can handle your complex pay rules, applying the correct rate (onshore/offshore, UK/Taiwan) to the job, saving "multiple days" of admin.
  • Integrated Tender Management: Manage your entire tender pipeline, log every client interaction, and set automated follow-up tasks to ensure you win more high-value contracts.
  • Unified Compliance Hub: All operative training and certifications (e.g., GWO) are managed in one profile, with automated expiry alerts, creating a single, auditable compliance record.
  • Built-in Digital Forms: Replace iAuditor. Build your RAMS, rescue plans, and site inspections directly in BigChange. Force operatives to complete them before starting work, creating a perfect, instant audit trail.

Your Blueprint for a Scalable Global Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, feature by feature.

Automated Timesheets & Complex Pay

The Pain: "one to two full-time roles" wasted on manual timesheets. Our app captures all operative time. We can then use our Automation Builder to handle your complex pay logic (onshore/offshore, day/night, Taiwan uplift) to save "multiple days" of admin.

Long-Term Project Scheduling

The Pain: Managing 1-week to 2-year jobs on spreadsheets. Use our 'Group Jobs' feature to plan multi-year projects, see all operatives (UK, US, Taiwan) on one schedule, and manage resource availability.

Unified Tender & CRM

The Pain: "CRM in a spreadsheet". Replace your spreadsheet with a live pipeline. Manage your multi-year tender process, log all client interactions, and set automated follow-up tasks to ensure you win more high-value contracts.

Replace iAuditor & AdeptSoft

The Pain: Clunky, disconnected compliance. Build your digital forms (Inspections, RAMS, Toolbox Talks, Rescue Plans) directly in BigChange. Link them to the job and force operatives to complete them, creating a perfect, instant audit trail.

Holistic Workforce Management

The Pain: No central hub for operatives. Use BigChange as your single portal. Operatives can request leave, access project documents (RAMS, plans), and view their timesheets—all from one app, anywhere in the world.

API-Ready for Your ERP

The Pain: Future-proofing for an ERP. BigChange is built to integrate. Use our powerful RESTful APIs to connect BigChange as the operational arm of your new ERP, ensuring all your systems speak to each other seamlessly.
