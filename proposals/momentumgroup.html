Meeting 1: Discovery

Escaping the "Spreadsheet Fatigue"

Attendees: Nathan, Susie, Serge.

Momentum FM has grown significantly, but operations are still relying on manual processes suitable for a much smaller business. Key pain points identified:

  • Scheduling via WhatsApp: Taking photos of Excel screens to send rotas to engineers is inefficient and prone to error when things change.
  • PPM Nightmare: Managing PPMs across 20 buildings involves manually checking 20 separate Excel tabs and self-made calendars.
  • Information Black Hole: It takes up to 20 minutes to answer simple client queries like "When is the next lift service due?"
  • Lack of Asset History: No easy way to track remedial works or historical maintenance on specific assets (e.g., Lifts at Temple Square).

Current State vs. Future State

Transitioning from manual data entry to a unified digital platform.

Current State (Spreadsheets)

  • Scheduling Chaos: Rotas managed in Excel and shared via photos on WhatsApp. Changes require manual re-work and re-sending.
  • PPM Blind Spots: 20 separate tabs for 20 buildings. Managing compliance is a manual "tick box" exercise with high risk of error.
  • Slow Reactions: Finding information takes 20+ minutes per query, slowing down the Helpdesk.
  • Disconnected Teams: Information sits in silos—Safety Culture for inspections, Excel for dates, SharePoint for docs.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Digital Scheduling: Drag-and-drop diary updates engineers' tablets instantly. No more WhatsApp photos.
  • Automated PPMs: Recurring jobs auto-populate the diary. View compliance across all 20 buildings in a single dashboard.
  • Instant Insight: Search "Temple Square" and see every asset, every past job, and every upcoming service instantly.
  • Unified Workflow: Risk assessments, vehicle checks, and job sheets all in one app. Remedial works flagged immediately.
