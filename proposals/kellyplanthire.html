Your "Information Goes in Once" Solution

Moving from a fragmented, multi-system process to a single, streamlined workflow.

Current State: Paper, Excel, WhatsApp & Syrinx

  • Manual Scheduling: Schedule built in Excel, then manually sent to drivers via WhatsApp. No live view of progress or availability.
  • Paper-Based Field Work: Drivers use paper timesheets and collect paper tip tickets. They then take photos and send them via WhatsApp.
  • Chasing Paper: Office team "forever chasing" drivers for signed paper timesheets and tip tickets, which are pre-requisites for invoicing.
  • Manual Data Re-Entry: All data from paper timesheets and tip tickets is manually entered into Syrinx just to create a basic invoice.
  • Disconnected Accounts: Invoices from Syrinx are then manually imported into Sage 50. Information is handled at least 5 times.

Future State with BigChange

  • Live Digital Scheduling: One central, drag-and-drop schedule. Jobs are sent instantly to the driver's app, and the office sees live status updates (On Route, On Site, Done).
  • All-in-One Digital Workflow: Drivers use one app to get jobs, complete digital timesheets, and take mandatory photos of tip tickets, attaching them directly to the job.
  • Instant Proof of Service: The moment a job is finished, the office has the signed timesheet and all tip tickets attached, ready for invoicing. No chasing, no lost paper.
  • Elimination of Syrinx: BigChange replaces this step entirely. Job data (hours, tips, etc.) flows from the app to a digital job sheet, ready to be invoiced from BigChange.
  • Seamless Sage 50 Integration: Invoices created in BigChange are pushed directly into Sage 50 via our Platinum Partner integration. Information is entered ONCE.

Your Blueprint for a Scalable Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, feature by feature.

End-to-End Digital Timesheets

The Pain: "Paper gets lost". Drivers complete digital timesheets on their app, get a customer signature, and submit. The office receives it instantly, ending the "very time consuming" process of chasing and sorting paper.

Mandatory Tip Ticket Capture

The Pain: "Can't invoice without the tip ticket". We make it a mandatory step. The driver *must* take a photo of the tip ticket on the app before they can complete the job, guaranteeing you have the proof you need to bill.

Replace Syrinx & Integrate Sage

The Pain: Multiple disconnected systems. BigChange replaces your Excel, WhatsApp, and Syrinx processes. We provide the schedule, the driver app, and the invoicing platform, which then feeds directly into Sage 50.

Live, Drag-and-Drop Schedule

The Pain: Static Excel spreadsheet. Manage your 40 sweepers from a live, visual planner. See who is available, who is on a job, and who is VOR. Drag and drop to schedule or reschedule jobs in seconds.

Full Fleet & Compliance

The Pain: Managing the fleet. See all your trucks on one map. Get automated alerts for MOT, tax, and service intervals. Mandate daily vehicle checks on the app and get instant VOR alerts in the workshop.

Accurate Quoting & Rates

The Pain: Manual rate emails. Create professional, branded quotes from the system. Set up different rates (half-load, full-load, per-tonne tips) for different customers to ensure 100% accurate billing every time.
