Gallagher Bassett & BigChange
Meeting Outcomes & ROI – Streamlining Field Service Operations
Key Customer Pain Points
Manual and inconsistent technician setup, with unclear license allocation and skills tracking.
Fragmented job management workflow, making it difficult to track jobs from quote to invoice and report accurately across regions.
Limited mobile capabilities for technicians, lacking real-time job completion tracking and legal compliance checks (e.g., vehicle checks).
Need for practical, accessible training resources for staff onboarding and upskilling.
Uncertainty around implementation timelines and resource allocation for insurance company setups.
BigChange Solutions
Automated CRM module with standardized customer prefixing and parent-child account structure for streamlined account creation.
Centralized technician setup with integrated skills tracking (e.g., asbestos training) and pending license allocation for future flexibility.
End-to-end job management workflow: from quoting to invoicing, with categorized job types and regional reporting for operational clarity.
Advanced mobile app: job acceptance, vehicle checks for legal compliance, conditional worksheets, photo capture, and drawing tools for comprehensive job completion.
Access to BigChange University for practical, on-demand training and upskilling of staff.
Clear project milestones and go-live date, ensuring timely delivery and resource planning.
Projected ROI for Gallagher Bassett
Time Savings: 25% reduction in admin time per job (from streamlined workflows and mobile app)
Compliance: 100% vehicle checks logged, reducing legal risk
Training: 50% faster onboarding via BigChange University
Reporting: Real-time, accurate regional job data for better decision-making
Est. £75,000+ annual savings
(Based on reduced admin hours, compliance risk mitigation, and improved operational efficiency)
