Gallagher Bassett & BigChange

Meeting Outcomes & Value Proposition
Key Customer Pain Points
Manual and inconsistent job management processes across regions, leading to reporting gaps.
Lack of real-time skills tracking for technician compliance (e.g., asbestos training).
Uncertainty and delays in vehicle check compliance, risking legal liability.
Onboarding and training challenges for new users, with limited practical resources.
BigChange Solutions
Automated, end-to-end job workflow from quote to invoice, with customizable job types for granular regional reporting.
Technician profiles with integrated skills tracking and license management for full compliance visibility.
Mobile app with mandatory vehicle checks and digital audit trail, reducing legal risk.
Access to BigChange University for hands-on, practical training and faster user adoption.

£42,000+ Annual Savings

  • Fewer compliance breaches & legal risks (est. £9,000/year)
  • Faster onboarding & fewer training hours (est. £5,000/year)
