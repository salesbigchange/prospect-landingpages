BigChange's Story & Timeline

From Startup to Industry Leader - A Journey of Innovation & Growth

Founded by Martin Port

2013

Martin Port, a serial entrepreneur from Leeds, founded BigChange with the goal of revolutionising the field service management industry, bringing together job management, CRM and vehicle tracking in one native platform.

Rapid Growth

2013-2020

BigChange experienced explosive growth - scaling from launch to over $30 million in annual recurring revenue by 2020, and acquiring around 2000 customers by that point.

GHP Investment

2021

In February 2021, BigChange received a £75 million growth investment from Boston-based private equity firm Great Hill Partners, valuing the business at around £100 million.

K1 Partners Investment

2024

In November 2024, BigChange was acquired by Simpro Group, backed by K1 Investment Management—marking a major milestone in its growth journey as part of a powerful portfolio of field service software solutions.

Built for Scale: How We Migrate & Protect

Data Migration

We understand that migrating your existing data can be daunting. We’ve built a streamlined and proven approach to ensure a smooth transition. Key operational data can be imported efficiently into BigChange using pre-defined Excel templates.

Our experienced onboarding team provide hands-on guidance and validation throughout the process. Whether you're consolidating systems or starting from spreadsheets, our structured approach ensures minimal disruption and maximum data integrity.

Security

BigChange treats the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data as paramount. Our Information Security Management System (ISMS) is certified to ISO 27001, and we align with additional standards such as ISO 22301 for Business Continuity.
