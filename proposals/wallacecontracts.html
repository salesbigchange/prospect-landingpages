Pain & Challenges

edit_document

Over-Reliance on Spreadsheets

Every aspect of your operation, from quoting and scheduling to project tracking and timesheets, is managed in separate, disconnected Excel files. This is labour-intensive and creates a high risk of human error.

sync_problem

Duplicated Data Entry

Information is manually copied and pasted between multiple spreadsheets. Job numbers, employee details, and project costs are entered multiple times, wasting 3-5 hours of admin time per week.

visibility_off

Lack of Real-Time Visibility

With no live connection between the office and the field, you lack a real-time view of job progress, operative locations, and project profitability, making it difficult to react quickly to issues.

rule

Compliance & Safety Gaps

Relying on a separate app for vehicle checks and RAMS means there's no way to force completion before a job starts. Chasing operatives for this paperwork is inefficient and creates a compliance risk.
