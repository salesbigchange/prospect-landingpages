Current State vs. Future State

Mapping Inside Contracts' identified pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with Manual Processes

  • Fragmented Communication: Job requests arrive via phone, WhatsApp, and email, leading to a lack of transparency and a high risk of missed jobs. 1
  • Spreadsheet Chaos: Operations are managed across multiple disconnected spreadsheets for booking, scheduling, invoicing, and pricing, causing massive inefficiencies. 1
  • Paper-Based & Slow: Paper sign-off sheets are physically collected or photographed, delaying data entry, creating paperwork backlogs, and slowing down the entire invoicing cycle. 1
  • Reactive Scheduling: Constant job changes from builders (3-4 times a day) are hard to manage on a spreadsheet, leading to scheduling errors and frustration. 1
  • Complex Pricing & VAT: Different pricing for each client, multiple flooring options, and complex VAT rules require manual calculations and separate invoicing, increasing the risk of errors. 1

Future State with BigChange

  • A Single Source of Truth: All job requests are logged in one system, providing a complete, time-stamped audit trail and eliminating disputes.
  • Unified & Automated Workflow: A single platform that connects booking, scheduling, pricing, job completion, and invoicing, cutting out duplicate data entry entirely.
  • Instant Digital Sign-offs: Field staff capture photos and signatures on the app, which are instantly available to the office, allowing for same-day invoicing and improved cash flow.
  • Dynamic Drag-and-Drop Diary: Easily manage schedule changes in a live, visual calendar. Automated alerts keep everyone in the loop, reducing errors and stress.
  • Automated & Accurate Billing: Pre-defined rate cards and a product catalogue handle complex pricing and VAT rules automatically, ensuring every invoice is correct.
