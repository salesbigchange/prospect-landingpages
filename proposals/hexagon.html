Meeting 1: Discovery

Unifying the Fragmented Workflow

Attendees: Daniel Szad (Hexagon), Serge, Ollie.

Hexagon Developments is a growing insurance contractor managing reinstatement works for major players like Aviva and LV. Key pain points identified:

  • The Spreadsheet Maze: Managing 50+ workers via online calendars and manual spreadsheets is creating "capital leakage" through unmeasured time.
  • Subcontractor Complexity: 35 subbies (working full-time) require better management of job reports, photos, and compliance.
  • Manual Admin Burden: The office is "jumping between systems" (Bright HR, calendars, spreadsheets) which is time-consuming and prone to errors.
  • Compliance & Safety: Need a rigorous way to ensure Health & Safety (risk assessments) and Proof of Presence (geolocation) are logged for every job.

Current State vs. Future State

Moving from a "manual mess" to a unified enterprise solution.

Current State (Manual & Fragmented)

  • System Hopping: Jumping between Bright HR for holidays, Google Calendars for scheduling, and Excel for financials.
  • Manual Tracking: Relying on trust for arrival/departure times without hard geolocation evidence.
  • Disconnected Photos: Photos sent via email or uploaded separately, often requiring manual matching to claims.
  • Admin Overload: Office staff spending hours manually re-keying data for insurers and processing subbie invoices.

Future State (Unstoppable with BigChange)

  • All-in-One CRM: Holidays, scheduling, tracking, and job reporting in one single, searchable platform.
  • Proof of Presence: Automated geolocation stamps show exactly where the guys are when they press "Start Job".
  • Automated Job Reports: High-quality PDF reports (with Hexagon logo) generated instantly from the field.
  • Financial Clarity: Real-time margin tracking per project, comparing planned vs. actual labor costs automatically.
