Current State with Paper & Spreadsheets

  • Multi-Company "Stumbling Block": No single system can handle invoicing from three separate legal entities, forcing complex workarounds or costly separate systems.
  • Paper-Based Job Dispatch: Supervisors manually create and hand out physical job sheets every morning. No live view of job progress.
  • Complex Scheduling Nightmare: Multi-day, multi-stage, and reactive fault jobs are managed manually, leading to inefficiency and errors.
  • Uncontrolled Schedule Visibility: Engineers can see their schedule far in advance, allowing them to "cherry-pick" jobs by calling in sick.
  • Risky Compliance: Paper-based risk assessments and no way to enforce photo capture for UKPN jobs, creating a significant compliance and financial risk.

Future State with BigChange

  • Unified Multi-Company Invoicing: One system to manage all three businesses. A custom 'Invoicing Company' field on each job ensures work is billed from the correct entity (Dorling Ltd, J&J Drake Ltd, or Drake Properties). This eliminates confusion and allows for clean, separate financial reporting for each business.
  • 100% Digital Job Management: Jobs are sent instantly to the engineer's device, complete with all site history, SOR codes, and required H&S forms.
  • Powerful "Group Jobs": Easily manage complex projects. A "Group Job" (e.g., 'Potter Heigham Substation') can contain multiple sub-jobs (Civils, Electrical) invoiced by different companies.
  • Controlled Schedule Visibility: You control what engineers see. Limit their job view to only 24-48 hours in advance, eliminating the "sick day" problem and giving you true planning flexibility.
  • Mandatory Digital Compliance: Force engineers to complete digital risk assessments and mandate a *minimum* number of 'Before' and 'After' photos (up to 100 per visit) before a job can be completed.

Your Blueprint for a Scalable Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, feature by feature.

Unified Multi-Company Invoicing

The Pain: The "Stumbling Block". We solve this with a custom "Invoicing Company" field on each job. This allows you to run all three businesses from one platform and easily manage separate financial reporting and invoicing for each entity through Sage 50.

Complex & Reactive Scheduling

The Pain: Manual, complex job planning. Use our "Group Jobs" feature to manage multi-stage projects. Use our flexible scheduler to manage 365-day reactive fault work, with full visibility of all 40+ teams on one screen.

Controlled Schedule Visibility

The Pain: Engineers calling in sick. Solve this instantly. You can set engineer devices to only show their schedule 24 or 48 hours in advance, eliminating the "sick day" problem and giving you true planning flexibility.

Mandatory Digital Compliance

The Pain: Paper-based risk. Digitize 100% of your compliance. Force engineers to complete vehicle checks, RAMS, and risk assessments. Mandate a minimum of 5, 10, or a number photos before a job can be marked as complete.

UKPN Schedule of Rates (SORs)

The Pain: Manual quoting and coding. We will help you upload your UKPN and council SOR codes. This allows you to build accurate quotes in seconds and enables engineers to add the correct SOR codes on-site, ensuring 100% accurate invoicing.

Automated Holiday & Training

The Pain: Manual admin. Engineers request holidays via the app, and you approve them, which automatically blocks them from the schedule. We also provide a digital training matrix with expiry alerts for all critical certifications.
