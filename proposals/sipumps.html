Current State vs. Future State

Mapping SI Pumps' identified pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with Red Zebra

  • Operational Friction: Clunky system with poor internal adoption. Engineers struggle to fill in reports correctly, leading to inconsistent data. 1
  • Manual Reporting Nightmare: Downloading, converting, and shrinking PDFs for customers is a significant time drain and a "big pain". 1
  • System Limitations: Plagued by storage limits requiring manual deletion of attachments. The scheduling calendar is not fit for purpose and cannot handle the team's scale. 1
  • Disconnected Processes: Service reminders are tracked manually, relying on individuals to remember, leading to missed opportunities and inconsistent customer service. 1
  • Wasted Time: Multiple staff members downloading the same job file, with each download taking up to 5 minutes, crippling productivity. 1

Future State with BigChange

  • Unified Cloud Platform: A user-friendly, all-in-one system that works on any device, driving high user adoption and eliminating manual workarounds.
  • Instant, Professional Reporting: Job sheets and certificates are generated automatically and sent to clients with a single click, directly from the platform.
  • Dynamic, Scalable Scheduling: A visual, drag-and-drop scheduler provides a clear overview of all 20+ engineers, making resource planning simple and efficient.
  • Automated Customer Communications: Set-and-forget service reminders are sent automatically, improving customer retention. Proactive "engineer on the way" alerts with live tracking reduce inbound calls.
  • Seamless Sage 50 Integration: A deep, out-of-the-box integration with your existing Sage 50 accounting package streamlines the entire job-to-invoice process.
