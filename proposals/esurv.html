What We Heard on the BDR Call

A summary of the key pains and objectives discussed with Kate during our initial conversation.

Observed Pains

  • Inefficient Routing: Surveyors often cross paths or visit the same new housing estate on consecutive days.
  • Lack of Real-Time Data: The current system doesn't account for live traffic, roadworks, or allow for on-the-fly adjustments.
  • Poor Work-Life Balance: Schedules are sent late the night before, giving surveyors little agency over their work week and personal appointments.
  • Manual Mileage Claims: The process is a "nuisance", leading to surveyors not claiming what they're owed because it's "too much hassle".
    "A lot of our surveyors just don't claim the mileage because it's too much hassle."
  • Fragmented Mobile Workflow: Fieldwork on the Totality app requires follow-up work at a home office to complete reports.

Desired Outcomes

  • Smarter Optimisation: A system that creates efficient routes and prevents overlapping surveyor paths.
  • Improved Surveyor Experience: A primary driver is improving work-life balance by providing schedules further in advance.
  • Automated Admin: Eliminate manual tasks like the "lengthy" mileage claims process and timesheets to save time and ensure fairness.
  • "Go Greener": Demonstrate effective routing to lenders as part of Net Zero initiatives.
  • Flexible & Modern Software: Explore modular solutions that can integrate with existing systems and provide better, more modern functionality.

Hypotheses to Validate

Some initial thoughts we'd like to explore with you during our discovery session today.

  • Sub-optimal routing is leading to an estimated 10-15% excess mileage and is a source of frustration for surveyors.

    Validation Method: Review sample routes and show BigChange's dynamic, traffic-aware routing engine.

  • The lack of advance scheduling is a key factor impacting surveyor work-life balance and contributes to attrition.

    Validation Method: Discuss surveyor feedback and demonstrate multi-day scheduling and leave management in BigChange.

  • Automating mileage claims for the mixed fleet could save each surveyor 1-2 hours per month and resolve a significant fairness issue.

    Validation Method: Walk through automated mileage capture via tracking and its application for both fleet and allowance vehicles.

  • A flexible, modular job management solution could integrate with your core case management system to solve the routing problem without requiring a full replacement.

    Validation Method: Discuss our open API and show examples of how we integrate with bespoke ERP/CRM systems.
