Meeting 1: Discovery

Escaping the "Bespoke CRM Trap"

Attendees: Lianna Naylor (Senior Stairlifts), Serge, Lee.

Senior Stairlifts is growing rapidly, but your internal bespoke CRM build is "holding you back." Key pain points identified:

  • The Bespoke Bottleneck: Your internal software development isn't moving quick enough to match your growth.
  • Manual Reporting Hell: The Finance Director is manually checking jobs daily to see who sold warranties. This is unscalable.
  • Asset Blindspots: You need to know exactly which stairlift (make/model/serial) is at which property for servicing and buybacks.
  • Stock Leakage: No current system to track stock on vans or deduct it automatically when used on an install.

Current State vs. Future State

Moving from a "work in progress" to a finished enterprise solution.

Current State (Manual & Mixed)

  • Developer Dependency: Waiting on an internal developer to add features is slowing down your ability to operate efficiently.
  • Data Vacuum: You have to manually calculate engineer conversion rates and "overbook" stats.
  • Paper Hybrid: Quotes and surveys are often paper-based, photographed, and uploaded, creating dead data.
  • Blind Scheduling: Ops team manually decides routes without system intelligence on efficient routing.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Ready-to-Scale: A proven platform used by competitors like Leodes and EMS, ready to handle your growth today.
  • Automated Insights: Instant dashboards showing Engineer Leaderboards, Warranty Sales, and Profitability.
  • Full Asset DNA: Every stairlift installed becomes an asset record with full service history for future buybacks.
  • Smart Routing: "Scheduling Assistant" finds the most cost-effective engineer for every call-out instantly.

The Business Case for Senior Stairlifts

Why switching to BigChange drives value beyond just software.

Revenue & Sales

Upsell Tracking: Automatically track which engineer sold a warranty or upgrade on-site. No more Finance Director checking manually.

Quote Speed: Engineers can build and sign quotes digitally on-site, syncing instantly to the office for ordering.

Asset & Stock Control

Van Stock: Treat every van as a warehouse. When a part is used on a repair, it deducts from the van and adds cost to the job.

Asset History: Essential for your buyback model. Know the exact service history of a unit before you buy it back.

Operational Wins

  • Route Optimisation: Maximise your 15 engineers' day by automatically ordering their jobs based on location.
