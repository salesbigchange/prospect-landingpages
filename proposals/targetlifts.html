Current State vs. Future State

Mapping Target Lifts' operational pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with JobLogic

  • No Forward Visibility: Struggling to get a clear view of upcoming PPM jobs for the next month, making proactive planning and resource allocation difficult.
  • Financial Blindspots: Inability to see granular costs, forcing the use of manual spreadsheets to track profitability and budget variance for jobs and projects.
  • Disconnected Financials: Invoicing and purchasing are done manually in both JobLogic and Xero, doubling data entry and creating reconciliation headaches. The Xero integration is not trusted.
  • Clunky & Unintuitive App: The engineer's mobile app is described as "poor" and "far too busy," making it difficult for them to see outstanding jobs or asset history on site.
  • Messy & Inflexible Reporting: Extracting simple reports, like uninvoiced accepted quotes or jobs on a terminated contract, is described as "the hardest thing in the world".

Future State
with BigChange

  • Complete Planning Control: Instantly view all upcoming PPMs and scheduled jobs on a live list view, with smart scheduling that suggests the most cost-effective engineer.
  • Unified Financial Control: Link purchase orders directly to jobs and projects and track all associated costs in one place.
  • Seamless Xero Integration: A robust, two-way sync for invoices, purchase orders, and payments eliminates double entry and ensures your financial data is always accurate.
  • An App Engineers Love: An intuitive, user-friendly mobile app gives engineers a clear view of their day, full job history for each asset, and the ability to see other outstanding jobs nearby.
  • Powerful, Customisable Reporting: Create custom reports or integrate with Power BI through BigChange's DAAS offering to analyse everything from engineer performance to asset profitability.
  • Total Workforce Management: Manage engineer skills, qualifications, and expiry dates with automated alerts. Factor in absences and real-time availability for smarter scheduling.
  • Superior Customer Experience: Delight customers with automated "en-route" alerts and an "Uber-style" tracking map, providing a premium and transparent service journey.

  • Asset Data (Lifts): Your full database of 1200+ lifts, including make, model, type, and location.

  • Users & Vehicles: All office (web) users, field (app) users, and vehicle records to build your operational structure.

Functional Configuration

We will configure the platform to your specific business units, addressing your key pain points:

  • Financials: Granular job costing, linking POs and engineer time to jobs for true profitability tracking.
  • Xero Integration: A robust, two-way sync for invoices, POs, and payments to eliminate double entry.
