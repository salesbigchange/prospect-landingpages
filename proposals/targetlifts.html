Current State vs. Future State

Mapping Target Lifts' operational pains to tangible solutions within the BigChange platform.

Current State with JobLogic

  • No Forward Visibility: Struggling to get a clear view of upcoming PPM jobs for the next month, making proactive planning and resource allocation difficult.
  • Financial Blindspots: Inability to see granular costs, forcing the use of manual spreadsheets to track profitability and budget variance for jobs and projects.
  • Disconnected Financials: Invoicing and purchasing are done manually in both JobLogic and Xero, doubling data entry and creating reconciliation headaches. The Xero integration is not trusted.
  • Clunky & Unintuitive App: The engineer's mobile app is described as "poor" and "far too busy," making it difficult for them to see outstanding jobs or asset history on site.
  • Messy & Inflexible Reporting: Extracting simple reports, like uninvoiced accepted quotes or jobs on a terminated contract, is described as "the hardest thing in the world".

Future State
with BigChange

  • Complete Planning Control: Instantly view all upcoming PPMs and scheduled jobs on a live list view, with smart scheduling that suggests the most cost-effective engineer.
  • Unified Financial Control: Link purchase orders directly to jobs and projects and track all associated costs in one place.
  • Seamless Xero Integration: A robust, two-way sync for invoices, purchase orders, and payments eliminates double entry and ensures your financial data is always accurate.
  • An App Engineers Love: An intuitive, user-friendly mobile app gives engineers a clear view of their day, full job history for each asset, and the ability to see other outstanding jobs nearby.
  • Powerful, Customisable Reporting: Create custom reports or integrate with Power BI through BigChange's DAAS offering to analyse everything from engineer performance to asset profitability.
  • Total Workforce Management: Manage engineer skills, qualifications, and expiry dates with automated alerts. Factor in absences and real-time availability for smarter scheduling.
  • Superior Customer Experience: Delight customers with automated "en-route" alerts and an "Uber-style" tracking map, providing a premium and transparent service journey.

Key Implementation Streams

Data Migration Strategy

A clean data import is the foundation of your new system. We will provide you with import templates and guides to migrate:

  • Customers & Sites: All billing and service locations.
  • Asset Data (Lifts): Your full database of 1200+ lifts, including make, model, type, and location.
  • PPM Schedules: All recurring service contracts and visit frequencies to build your planning diary.
  • Users & Vehicles: All office (web) users, field (app) users, and vehicle records to build your operational structure.
  • Parts List: (If applicable) Your core parts list to enable purchasing and van stock management.

Functional Configuration

We will configure the platform to your specific business units, addressing your key pain points:

  • Job Workflows: Separate, tailored workflows for Service/PPM, Reactive Callouts, and large-scale Projects.
  • Financials: Granular job costing, linking POs and engineer time to jobs for true profitability tracking.
  • Xero Integration: A robust, two-way sync for invoices, POs, and payments to eliminate double entry.
  • End to End Process Build: We will collaboratively map and build your ideal end-to-end process within BigChange, incorporating the specific automations and workflows designed with our sales engineering team.

Training & Change Management

We use a "Train the Trainer" model to empower your Super Users, supported by our team and online resources.

  • Office Staff (Schedulers, Finance): Remote and on-site sessions focused on scheduling, financials, and reporting.
  • Engineers (Service & Projects): Mobile-focused training led by your Super Users, covering job management, forms, and asset history.
  • Leadership Team: 1-to-1 sessions on dashboards and Power BI reporting.
  • LMS Access: On-demand video training via our Learning Management System for all users.

Your Onboarding Journey: A Phased Approach to Success

1. Welcome to BigChange University (LMS)

Your journey begins with access to our on-demand Learning Management System (LMS), BigChange University. Before the formal project starts, all your users—from field to office—can complete modules at their own pace. This foundational learning covers the basics of the platform, terminology, and core navigation.

  • Key Activity: Self-paced e-learning for all users.
  • Deliverable: Your team arrives at the kick-off already speaking the language of BigChange, making the implementation workshops far more productive.

2. Discovery & Kick-Off

With the basics covered, we host the formal project kick-off. Your Senior Implementation Consultant will align with your key stakeholders on scope, goals, and timelines, and collaboratively map your exact Service and Project workflows.

  • Key Activity: Workflow Scoping Workshops
  • Deliverable: Signed-off Scope Document

3. Configuration & Data Build

This is the core build phase. We import your cleansed data (Assets, Customers, Sites) and configure the key modules, including your PPM schedules, custom forms, and the Xero integration. Your Super Users will be involved in User Acceptance Testing (UAT) to ensure everything works as expected.

  • Key Activity: Data Import & UAT
  • Deliverable: Fully configured test system signed off by Super Users.

4. Training & Go-Live

We empower your internal champions through a "Train the Trainer" program. Your Super Users, now experts, will lead the end-user training with our full support. We then execute a phased go-live, starting with the Service department, followed by Projects & Finance, with on-site "Floorwalking" support to ensure a smooth transition.

  • Key Activity: Train the Trainer & Phased Go-Live
  • Deliverable: All users trained and operating live on the BigChange platform.

5. Review, Optimise & Handover

After your first month live, we conduct a Project Review to measure success against your original goals (e.g., PPM visibility, financial reporting). We'll help you set up your Power BI reports and then formally hand you over to your dedicated Customer Success Manager, who will be your partner for continuous improvement and future growth.

  • Key Activity: Project Review & CSM Handover
  • Deliverable: A live, stable system and a long-term plan for optimisation.
