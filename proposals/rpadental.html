Current State of Play

apartment

Industry

RPA Dental is a key supplier of dental equipment, operating across three channels: direct sales, sub-dealers, and service operations, including warranty repairs and maintenance.

groups

Business Scale

The business supports approximately 20 staff, including 9 engineers and 11 back-office personnel, managing a complex inventory of over 15,000 items.

dns

Current Systems

Operations currently rely on Sage 200 and SICON (Cyclone). These systems are described as outdated, inefficient, and "clunky," creating data silos and hindering real-time reporting and collaboration.

Pain & Challenges

power_off

System Inefficiency

'Walking through treacle' feeling due to non-real-time data, closed period limitations, and overly complex processes.

inventory_2

Inventory Management Gaps

Managing 15,000+ SKUs exceeds the capacity of systems like Xero (4,000 limit), creating a critical operational gap.

edit_document

Manual & Paper Processes

The commercial team's reliance on paper consumes valuable management time. Manual currency conversions add further administrative burden.

lan

Lack of Integration

The SICON service system pulls outdated stock data from Sage 200, causing operational friction and risk of costly errors.

Complex Data Migration

Historical data from legacy systems is complex and cluttered, requiring a significant data cleansing effort before migration.

report_off

Poor Reporting Capabilities

Current systems offer poor reporting, hindering accountant collaboration and making it difficult to get a clear, real-time view of business performance.

Aims & Business Goals

hub

Create a Unified Ecosystem

Transition to a modern, integrated solution with Unleashed as the master for inventory, BigChange for job management, and Xero for accounting, creating a single source of truth.

sync

Achieve Seamless Integration

Implement bidirectional data flow between engineering usage in BigChange and warehouse updates in Unleashed, ensuring real-time stock accuracy across the business.

monitoring

Enhance Reporting & Insight

Eliminate cumbersome, manual reporting processes with a system that provides live, accurate data, enabling better financial and operational decision-making.

model_training

Digitise & Automate Workflows

Replace inefficient paper-based systems on the commercial side and automate processes like currency conversion to free up valuable team resources for client-facing activities.
