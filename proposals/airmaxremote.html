Meeting 1: Discovery

Moving from Legacy to Innovation

Attendees: Dave (Head of Eng), Tom (Lead Eng), Megan (Finance), Connor (Finance), Seemaab (Tech Support), Thomas (Customer Support), Serge.

Airmax Remote needs a robust system to replace the outdated in-house web tool. Key challenges identified:

  • Legacy System: The current in-house URL system logs jobs but lacks modern integration and is "quite outdated."
  • Supply Chain Disconnect: Partners like RS Connect already use BigChange, but current processes involve manual data entry.
  • Blue Light Complexity: Specialized installs for emergency services (police, ambulance) need strict, traceable workflows.
  • Finance Silo: Operations and Finance (NetSuite/Oracle) are not talking to each other automatically.

Current State vs. Future State

Transitioning from disparate systems to a unified, scalable platform.

Current State (Legacy & Manual)

  • Legacy In-House System: An outdated web link system that isolates data and requires manual updates.
  • Manual Handovers: Sending jobs to partners like RS Connect involves email and double-keying data.
  • Stock Blind Spots: Difficult to track high-value stock (trackers, cameras) assigned to in-house engineers.
  • Paper/Digital Mix: A mix of digital forms and paper job sheets creates inconsistency in audit trails.

Future State (BigChange)

  • BigChange Network: Seamlessly share jobs with RS Connect at the click of a button. No more double entry.
  • NetSuite Integration: Use our open APIs to push financial data directly to your Oracle/NetSuite ERP.
  • Live Stock Control: Track exactly which engineer has what stock (serials, models) on their van in real-time.
  • Blue Light Workflows: Mandatory, customized workflows (barcode scanning, dash photos) ensure 100% compliance for police force installs.

The Business Case for Airmax

Why integrating your operations is critical for efficiency and growth.

The Efficiency Gap

Double Handling: Creating jobs in your legacy system and then manually sending them to partners wastes valuable time.

Visibility: Without a centralized map, tracking nationwide jobs for emergency services is difficult.

Stock Loss Risk: Without live van stock management, expensive telematics kit is harder to account for.

Key Goals

Interconnectivity: A system that talks to everyone—your in-house team, your subcontractors (RS Connect), and your finance software.

Compliance: Bulletproof audit trails for Blue Light installations.

Future Proofing: Moving away from legacy in-house tools to a supported, constantly evolving platform.

Why BigChange?

  • The Network: You can electronically "handshake" with RS Connect to share jobs instantly.
  • API Capability: Robust, open APIs allow your developers to link BigChange finance data directly to NetSuite.
  • Custom Workflows: Build specific "Defit" or "Install" workflows that mandate barcode scanning and photos.
