Meeting 1: Discovery

Moving to the "21st Century"

Attendees: Jamie (Engineering Dir), Mason (IT), Scott, Amy, Serge.

APL is looking to modernize its operations, moving away from disparate systems and manual processes. Key pain points identified:

  • Spreadsheet Fatigue: "Normal lots" of time wasted manually entering data from Insphire into Excel to build reports.
  • The Insphire Silo: Insphire is great for rental, but it doesn't talk back. Workshop jobs and damage reports are stuck in a one-way street.
  • No Real CRM: Currently relying on notes and spreadsheets, with no centralized view of customer interactions.
  • Sales Agility: Sales reps need to quote on the fly from mobile devices, not wait to access a desktop system.

Current State vs. Future State

From "millions of systems that don't talk" to a unified, data-rich platform.

Current State (Manual & Fragmented)

  • Separate Tracking: Paying for FleetSmart but tracking sits outside of job planning.
  • Manual Reporting: Mason has to export data to Excel to query it, just to build basic reports.
  • Blind Spots: Transport and Engineering don't see the full picture of customer interactions (no CRM).
  • Static Quoting: Relying on desktop-based systems limits sales responsiveness on the road.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Unified Operations: CRM, Tracking, and Job Management in one login. "Nearest Engineer" visible instantly.
  • DAS & Power BI: All data pushes to Snowflake, feeding Power BI directly. No more manual exports for Mason.
  • Smart Transport: "Schedule All" feature automates route planning for your 25+ drivers/engineers.
  • Driver-Centric Design: Easy-to-use app for deliveries, collections, and call outs with mandatory workflows ensures 100% compliance and rapid adoption.
