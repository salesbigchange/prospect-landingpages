The High Cost of a Mismatched System

Simpro was meant to streamline operations, but its project-first design has created the opposite effect for your reactive-heavy business, leading to costly duplication and inefficiency.

~10 Hours/Week

Wasted by your helpdesk team fighting Simpro's clunky, multi-click scheduling process for ~50 reactive jobs a day. The inability to forward plan without assigning an engineer adds another layer of complexity and time.

From Project-First to Reactive-Ready

Moving from a system you have to work around, to a system that works for you.

Current State with Simpro

  • Rigid Scheduling: Jobs cannot be planned for a day without being assigned to a specific engineer, making forward planning for reactive work impossible.
  • Painful Materials Process: Engineers cannot add one-time items on the job, forcing a "ludicrous" photo-based workaround that leads to missed billing.
  • Fragmented Mobile Apps: Engineers have to switch between the main Simpro app and a separate 'Forms' app, creating a disjointed and confusing user experience.
  • Double-Entry Invoicing: A weak Xero integration forces the finance team to manually duplicate invoice information, wasting time and increasing the risk of errors.
  • Siloed Divisions: No viable way to manage both the maintenance and cleaning divisions within one system, forcing a choice between two separate platforms or excluding one division entirely.

Future State with BigChange

  • Flexible Reactive Scheduling: Plan jobs in an 'unscheduled' pot for a specific day or week, then simply drag-and-drop them to engineers as capacity becomes clear.
  • One-Touch Materials & Expenses: Engineers can instantly add one-time materials, parts, and expenses directly on the job with just a few taps, ensuring everything is captured and billed.
  • A Single, Unified App: One app for everything. Jobs, forms, timesheets, vehicle checks, expenses, and holiday requests are all in one intuitive interface.
  • Seamless Xero Integration: A true, platinum-partner integration. Invoices raised in BigChange are instantly and automatically created in Xero, with all line items and details included.
  • One Platform for All Divisions: Manage maintenance and cleaning teams side-by-side. Use roles and restrictions to ensure schedulers and managers only see the teams and jobs relevant to them.

Your Blueprint for a Scalable Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, feature by feature.

The Pain: Clunky Scheduling

The BigChange Solution: Unscheduled Job Pot

Our scheduler is built for reactive maintenance. Drop new jobs into a category-based 'unscheduled' list for the day. See all your resources and jobs on one screen, then drag-and-drop to allocate work in seconds.

The Pain: Leaking Materials Revenue

The BigChange Solution: In-App Expenses

Engineers can add parts bought from a supplier as a simple expense on the job. They enter the description, quantity, and cost, and take a photo of the receipt. It's captured instantly and ready to be billed.

The Pain: Disconnected Finance

The BigChange Solution: Platinum Xero Integration

When you raise an invoice in BigChange, it's created in Xero in real-time. Mark it as paid in Xero, and it updates in BigChange. It's a true two-way sync that eliminates double-entry for good.

The Pain: Cumbersome App Experience

The BigChange Solution: One Unified Mobile App

Forget switching between apps. Our single, intuitive app handles everything: receiving jobs, completing dynamic worksheets, managing van stock, submitting timesheets, and even requesting holidays.

The Pain: Divided Business View

The BigChange Solution: Roles & Restrictions

Run your maintenance and cleaning divisions from one platform. Custom user roles mean a cleaning supervisor only sees cleaning staff and jobs, while directors can see a unified view of the entire operation.

The Pain: Manual Holiday Process

The BigChange Solution: Integrated HR Features

Engineers request holidays through the app. Managers get a notification, see a team availability view, and approve or deny. Once approved, the engineer is automatically blocked out of the schedule for those dates.
