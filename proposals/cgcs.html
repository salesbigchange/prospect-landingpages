Current State vs. Future State

The transformation from optional workflows to forced accountability.

Current State (C&G)

  • Optional Steps: Engineers can add photos or notes without being on site or starting the job.
  • Manual Compliance: Risk assessments are frequently missed or done half-heartedly after the fact.
  • Payroll Blindness: No way to verify true return-to-base times vs. actual work hours.
  • Vehicle Tracking Split: Tracking is separate from the job sheet, leading to data silos.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Forced Process: "You can't go further until you do your RAMS." Linear, step-by-step app experience.
  • Guided Worksheets: Photos must be taken as part of the job flow (annotated and time-stamped).
  • Integrated Fleet: Free trackers installed in 20 vans. Live traffic updates and precise Eta's for CBRE/clients.
  • Sage 50 Integration: Automated financial flow from job completion to invoice sync.
