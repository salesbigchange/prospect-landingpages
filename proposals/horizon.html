Current State vs. Future State

Addressing the operational variance across the Vadella group with a unified, best-practice solution.

Current State: Disparate Systems

  • System Inconsistency: Acquired businesses range from having no systems (spreadsheets) to bespoke, optimised platforms (EvoMX), creating a fragmented tech landscape.
  • Reporting Challenges: A lack of a unified data structure makes group-level reporting and KPI tracking difficult, requiring manual data consolidation for PE backers.
  • Scalability Issues: The current mix of systems doesn't provide a scalable "cookie-cutter" model to quickly integrate new acquisitions and enforce best practices.
  • Variable Workflows: Each business operates with distinct processes for job management, from basic transactional work to more complex, multi-stage compliance reporting.

Future State with BigChange

  • Standardised Platform: A single, configurable platform to be rolled out across the group, providing a consistent operational blueprint for all acquisitions.
  • Group-Level Visibility: Utilise BigChange's Data as a Service (DAS) to feed real-time data from all business units into a central Power BI dashboard for comprehensive group reporting.
  • Rapid Onboarding for Acquisitions: A proven, templated implementation process allows new businesses to be integrated quickly and efficiently onto the group's standard operating platform.
  • Flexible & Configurable Workflows: BigChange's highly configurable nature supports the specific needs of each vertical (lightning protection, water treatment, etc.) within one system.

The Group Business Case

Key drivers for selecting a strategic platform partner for the Vadella group.

Metrics

Group Strategy: Buy and build in the Test, Inspection, and Compliance (TIC) sector.

Verticals: Lightning protection, height safety, water treatment, hazardous materials, electrical & mechanical.

Growth Target: £100M turnover and £25M EBITDA.

Decision Makers

Key Stakeholders: Neal Holden (Group), Richard (Group CFO), Lee & Neil (Horizon).

Key Outcomes: Find a scalable, out-of-the-box platform that can handle the operational diversity of the group, provide robust group-level reporting for PE backers, and be implemented efficiently in new acquisitions.

Decision Criteria

  • Must be a true "CRM to invoice" platform.
  • Ability to handle both simple and intricate job workflows.
  • Powerful reporting capabilities, with an option for group-level BI dashboards.
  • A proven, supportive implementation process (not just training).
  • A single platform that can grow with the group and adapt to new service lines.
