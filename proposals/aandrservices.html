Current State vs. Future State

Moving from a system that "just has the basics" to a system that drives efficiency.

Current State (E-Works)

  • Timesheet Nightmare: Ellie spends 0.5 - 2 days per week manually reconciling tracker data against timesheets.
  • Manual Quoting: Dan has to manually calculate prices and build quotes ("bulky bits"), leading to wasted time.
  • Job Black Holes: Unhelpful statuses in E-Works mean jobs "disappear" into buckets (like Overdue) and get missed.
  • Spreadsheet Reliance: You're duplicating work by maintaining separate allocation spreadsheets because E-Works scheduling isn't good enough.
  • Multi-Trade Issues: If a roofer finishes but internal damp work is needed, the job gets marked complete and the next stage stalls.

Future State (BigChange)

  • Automated Timesheets: Timesheets auto-reconcile with tracking data. Ellie gets 2 days a week back instantly.
  • One-Click Quoting: Use "Sets" and pick-lists (e.g., Flooring Templates) to build quotes instantly. Duplicate old quotes and adjust quantities in seconds.
  • Intelligent Workflows: Custom flags (e.g., "Awaiting Parts") and automated follow-on jobs ensure multi-trade projects never stall.
  • Green King Ready: Export data directly into the specific formats Green King requires, or use custom templates.
  • Forced Process: Engineers must complete vehicle checks and notes before starting work. No more empty job cards.
