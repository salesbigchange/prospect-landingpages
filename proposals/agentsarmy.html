Current State with Legacy System

  • Manual Booking Process: All incoming work orders from agents arrive as PDFs and must be manually cut and pasted into your system, a time-consuming and error-prone process.
  • No Client Portal: Estate agents have no way to self-serve. They must call or email for bookings, job updates, photo reports, and invoices, creating a bottleneck in your office.
  • Fragmented Field Communication: Job details are sent via email, while day-to-day communication happens on WhatsApp, leading to missed information and a lack of a central audit trail.
  • High "No Access" Rate: Your team spends hours every day calling to confirm appointments, yet still suffers from a high fall-through rate, wasting valuable team time and fuel.
  • Limited App Functionality: The field team's app is a single-use tool for photos only, offering no job management, timesheet, or communication capabilities.

Future State with BigChange

  • Automated Job Creation: Integrate directly with agent systems like Fixflo, or parse PDF work orders automatically, creating jobs in seconds and eliminating manual data entry.
  • 24/7 Client Self-Service Portal: Empower agents to book cleans, track job progress, view/download photo reports, and access historical invoices online, anytime, enhancing your value proposition.
  • All-in-One Mobile App: A single app for everything: receiving jobs, messaging the office, completing photo reports, timesheets, and vehicle checks. A unified hub for your field teams.
  • Automated Client Confirmations: Drastically reduce "no access" jobs with automated email/SMS reminders, booking confirmations, and "on-the-way" alerts with live ETA tracking.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Easily run reports to identify your most profitable agents, track recall rates per cleaning team, and analyse job profitability to make smarter business decisions.

Your Blueprint for a Scalable Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, feature by feature.

The Pain: Manual Work Orders

The BigChange Solution: Job Intake Automation

Our partners can build a direct, two-way integration with major agent platforms like Fixflo. For others, we can set up an email parsing tool that reads PDF work orders and automatically creates jobs in BigChange.

The Pain: Constant Client Queries

The BigChange Solution: Branded Client Portal

Give your agents a professional, easy-to-use portal to book jobs, track progress, and access all their historical data, including photo reports and invoices. This becomes a powerful USP for retaining and winning clients.

The Pain: High Fall-Through Rate

The BigChange Solution: Automated Communications

Set up a chain of automated alerts: a booking confirmation, a 24-hour reminder, and an "on-the-way" text with an Uber-style tracking link. Drastically reduce no-access appointments and wasted journeys.

The Pain: Disconnected Field Teams

The BigChange Solution: A True All-in-One App

One app to run their day. Teams receive jobs, follow structured photo workflows, message the office, and complete timesheets in one place. No more switching between emails, WhatsApp, and a separate photo app.

The Pain: Lack of Performance Data

The BigChange Solution: Powerful Reporting Suite

Create custom reports in minutes. Track your recall rate per team to identify training needs. Analyse job profitability by agent to understand your most valuable clients. Turn your data into actionable insights.

The Pain: Inflexible Invoicing

The BigChange Solution: Dynamic Pricing & Upsells

Set up variable pricing based on property size (e.g., 'EOT Clean - 2 Bed'). Empower teams to add predefined upsells like 'Carpet Steam Clean' on the job, ensuring all work is accurately captured and invoiced.
