Current State with Kickserv

  • Broken Xero Integration: Manually exporting PDFs to Dext and re-keying all invoice data into Xero, creating enormous admin overhead and financial data silos.
  • No Departmental Visibility: Managers for Drainage, Roofing, and Civils cannot filter to see their own team's jobs, leading to a "mess" of over 150 jobs a day in one unfiltered view.
  • Manual KPI Tracking: A dedicated employee spends their time manually exporting data to Excel just to track basic metrics like SLA compliance and first-time fix rates.
  • No Stock Control: Zero visibility of van stock. Finance staff must manually read through job notes to identify used parts and add them as expenses, a slow and unreliable process.
  • Paper-Based Compliance: Risk assessments are done on paper, photographed, and manually uploaded, a clunky workflow that lacks standardisation and is difficult to audit.

Future State with BigChange

  • Seamless Xero Integration: A true, platinum-partner two-way sync. Invoices raised in BigChange instantly appear in Xero with the correct customer data, and payments are reconciled automatically.
  • Departmental Workstreams: Managers can see a filtered view of just their team's engineers and their unallocated jobs, allowing them to plan, schedule, and manage their department effectively.
  • Live KPI Dashboards: All data is tracked in real-time. SLA performance, job profitability, and first-time fix rates are available in live dashboards, eliminating the need for manual spreadsheet reports.
  • Integrated Stock Management: Manage van and stores stock levels in real-time. When an engineer uses a part on a job, it's automatically deducted from their van and added to the job cost.
  • Digital, Forced Workflows: Mandate digital risk assessments before a job can start. All compliance is captured on the app with time and location stamps, creating a perfect, instant audit trail.

Your Blueprint for a Scalable Operation

Here’s exactly how BigChange resolves your core operational challenges, feature by feature.

Departmental Views

The Pain: Disjointed Scheduling. Create custom groups for your Drainage, Roofing, and Civils teams. Planners and managers can then view a filtered schedule showing only their engineers and their list of unallocated jobs, bringing clarity and focus.

Platinum Xero Integration

The Pain: Broken Financial Workflow. Our robust, two-way sync correctly links jobs for multi-site clients to a single parent account in Xero. Invoices and payments flow seamlessly, eliminating hours of daily data re-entry.

Live Dashboards & Alerts

The Pain: Manual Reporting. Track SLA performance and job profitability in real-time. Set up alerts to notify you of potential SLA breaches before they happen, allowing for proactive management instead of reactive reporting.

Integrated Stock & Parts

The Pain: No Stock Visibility. Manage stock levels across your stores and your entire fleet of 40 vans. Engineers can add parts used from van stock directly to a job, providing accurate job costing and simplifying stock replenishment.

Forced Digital Workflows

The Pain: Cumbersome Compliance. Make risk assessments a mandatory first step on every job. Engineers cannot start work until the digital form is completed, ensuring 100% compliance and creating a perfect audit trail.

SOR Codes & Quoting Tools

The Pain: Inefficient Quoting. Upload your clients' Schedule of Rates (SOR) codes directly into BigChange. Build quotes quickly and accurately, then convert them to jobs in a single click, with all financial data carrying over.
